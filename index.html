Biography

I am a year 2 PhD student at The Chinese university of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHKSZ), very fortunately to be supervised by Prof. Xiao Li. Before that, I obtained my bachelor degree in computer science from CUHKSZ in 2021.

My research objective is to develop efficient and explainable decision models. To this end, my current research interests broadly lie in the field of reinforcement learning (RL). In particular, I am actively exploring the following topics:

  • Optimality and optimization of RL
  • Conditions and algorithms for achieving generalization in RL
  • Inverse RL / RL with human feedback

I am actively looking for visiting oppotunities and collaborations. Feel free to drop me an email if you think my research background is a good match!
